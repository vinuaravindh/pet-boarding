About Me

Hello, I'm Sangeetha, a devoted pet lover residing in Sharjah. Pets have been an integral part of my life since childhood, and their care is not just an occupation for me, but a deeply cherished passion. As a stay-at-home mom who's experienced firsthand the challenge of finding a reliable pet sitter, I decided to offer a solution to this common problem by becoming one myself. My family shares my love for pets, with my children offering endless hugs and cuddles to our furry guests. Join me on this exciting journey and let's provide your pet with a loving, comfortable, and safe environment!

Getting Your Pet Ready for Their Staycation

Step 1: Reach Out to Me

Contact me to discuss your pet’s needs and schedule. We’ll ensure I'm the right fit for your pet’s stay. Get in touch.

Step 2: Schedule a Meet-and-Greet

Arrange a time to bring your pet over for a meet-and-greet. They can explore and get comfortable in their new temporary home while we discuss any special requirements.

Step 3: Book Your Dates

Once your pet feels right at home, book the dates for their stay. Rest assured that I’ll keep you updated with photos and videos.

Step 4: Drop Off and Relax

On the day of boarding, drop your pet off, and I’ll take care of the rest. Enjoy your time away knowing your pet is in safe hands.
